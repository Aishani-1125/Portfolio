Hi, I'm Aishani — a B.Tech Food Technology student at KL University with a deep passion for the science behind what we eat.

My academic journey has fueled my curiosity across the full spectrum of food science — from food processing and safety to nutritional biochemistry and quality control. I'm particularly drawn to how food research can directly impact human health and environmental sustainability.

I thrive at the intersection of laboratory science and real-world application — whether that means analyzing microbial risks in a HACCP framework, designing packaging to extend shelf life, or exploring functional foods that address modern nutrition challenges.

I believe the food industry's future belongs to those who combine scientific rigor with creative thinking. That's the kind of food technologist I'm working hard to become.
